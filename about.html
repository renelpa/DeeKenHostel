About DeeKen Hostel

Founded in the heart of Caramoan, DeeKen Hostel offers cozy accommodations, breathtaking views, and a gateway to unforgettable adventures.

Our Mission

To give every guest a warm Bicolano welcome, an affordable stay, and a memorable experience through nature and hospitality.

Our Vision

To be one of the top beach hostels in the Bicol Region, known for comfort, cleanliness, and community connection.
