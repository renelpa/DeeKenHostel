About DeeKen Hostel

DeeKen Hostel is a serene escape located in the beautiful town of Caramoan. Built with comfort and coastal charm in mind, we offer an affordable and relaxing place to stay for tourists, adventurers, and families.

Our Mission

To provide every guest a warm and memorable experience with personalized service, comfort, and genuine hospitality.

Our Vision

To be the most loved local hostel in Caramoan, where guests return for peace, nature, and Filipino warmth.

Why Choose Us?

  • Beachfront access
  • Island hopping tour assistance
  • Affordable and clean rooms
  • Free breakfast and WiFi
  • Local staff with a heart for service
