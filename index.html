Our Location

DeeKen Hostel is located in the heart of Caramoan, a beautiful town famous for its natural wonders. Surrounded by pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and towering limestone cliffs, Caramoan is the perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Some of the nearby attractions include:

  • Matukad Island: A hidden gem with stunning beaches and lush greenery.
  • Lahos Island: Known for its unique sandbars and crystal-clear waters.
  • Cagua Volcano: A challenging hike with breathtaking views from the top.
  • Caramoan National Park: A haven for wildlife enthusiasts and hikers.

Activities and Adventure

Caramoan is an adventure lover's paradise, and DeeKen Hostel is the perfect base for exploring the area. Here are some activities you can enjoy:

  • Island Hopping: Explore nearby islands, including Matukad and Lahos.
  • Snorkeling and Diving: Discover the vibrant marine life in the clear waters of Caramoan.
  • Hiking: Trek through the stunning Caramoan National Park or conquer Cagua Volcano.
  • Kayaking: Paddle through the peaceful waters and enjoy the surrounding scenery.
  • Historical Sites: Visit the Church of St. Michael the Archangel and other landmarks.
