Welcome to Caramoan!

Explore the stunning beaches, go island hopping, try local seafood delicacies, and enjoy nature adventures like caving and trekking!

  • Island Hopping: Visit Matukad Island's mystical lagoon, Lahos Island's twin beaches, and the expansive Manlawi Sandbar.
  • Beach Relaxation: Unwind on the white sands of Cotivas Beach and the serene shores of Lahuy Island.
  • Adventure Activities: Engage in rock climbing on Matukad Island, spelunking in Omang and Culapnit Caves, and kayaking along the coast.
  • Cultural Sites: Climb to the Our Lady of the Holy Rosary Mother of Peace Grotto atop Mt. Caglago and visit the historic St. Michael Archangel Church.
  • Local Cuisine: Savor traditional dishes like laing (taro leaves in coconut milk) and fresh seafood delicacies.
