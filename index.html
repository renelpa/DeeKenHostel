I Love Caramoan

Explore the beauty and adventure of Caramoan, Camarines Sur!

Things to Do in Caramoan

  • Island Hopping – Visit Manlawi Sandbar, Cagbalinad Island, Matukad Island.
  • Caving – Explore Lahuy Cave, Sabitang Laya Cave.
  • Snorkeling – Discover marine life at Gota Beach, Sabitang Laya.
  • Hiking Mt. Caglago – Trek to the peak with views of the islands.
  • Rock Formation Tours – See Matukad Rock, Sabitang Laya formations.
  • Local Culture & Cuisine – Enjoy Bicol Express, Laing, and fresh seafood.
  • Kayaking & Paddleboarding – In Bikal River and Matukad Island.
  • Visit the Caramoan National Park – Explore nature and wildlife.
