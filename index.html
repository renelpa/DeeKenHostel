Welcome to Caramoan!

Explore the stunning beaches, go island hopping, try local seafood delicacies, and enjoy nature adventures like caving and trekking!

Island Hopping

  • Matukad Island - mystical lagoon
  • Lahos Island - twin white beaches
  • Manlawi Sandbar - wide sandbar experience

Adventure Activities

  • Rock climbing on Matukad cliffs
  • Spelunking in Omang and Culapnit Caves
  • Kayaking and beach camping

Cultural and Food Experience

  • Visit Our Lady of the Holy Rosary Grotto
  • Historic St. Michael Church
  • Try Laing, Kinunot, and fresh seafood
